About Us

A few words about us

One of the best Platform to Change the Future of Agriculture Market

Krashi Bazar This platform provides different modules which make easy the procedure of agriculture marketing and help farmers and dealers to easily involve in digital harvest selling activities. The main objective of the proposed application is to build a website that will help the framer to get the best from his inputs. There will be the availability of up-to-date information on rates in various state Mandis. It gives choice to decide when and where to sell the product.
